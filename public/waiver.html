WAIVER, RELEASE OF LIABILITY & ASSUMPTION OF RISK

Please read carefully. This agreement affects your legal rights.

1. Your Participation & Understanding

By participating in classes with Red Maple Movement, you acknowledge that Pilates and fitness activities involve physical movement and exertion, and therefore carry inherent risks.

These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Muscle strains, sprains, or tears
  • Joint, back, neck, or spinal injuries
  • Dizziness, fainting, or loss of balance
  • Cardiovascular stress (including heart attack or stroke)
  • Aggravation of pre-existing conditions
  • Falls or minor equipment-related injuries

While every effort is made to provide a safe and supportive environment, injuries can still occur even when proper care is taken.

2. Health & Personal Responsibility

You confirm that you are physically able to participate in Pilates and fitness activities. You understand that it is your responsibility to:

  • Work within your limits
  • Inform your instructor of any injuries, conditions, or changes in your health
  • Modify or stop exercises if something does not feel right

You agree not to participate if you have any condition that could make exercise unsafe without appropriate medical clearance.

If you are pregnant, you confirm that you have consulted a qualified healthcare provider and have been cleared to participate.

Red Maple Movement instruction is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. You are encouraged to consult a healthcare professional before beginning a new exercise program.

3. Voluntary Assumption of Risk

By choosing to participate, you knowingly and voluntarily accept all risks associated with Pilates and fitness activities.

4. Release of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, you agree to release and hold harmless Red Maple Movement, its owner, instructors, and any associated staff or contractors from any claims, injuries, damages, or losses arising from your participation in classes or use of equipment.

This includes claims related to negligence. This release does not apply in cases of gross negligence or intentional harm.

5. Emergency Situations

In the event of an emergency, you authorize Red Maple Movement to contact emergency services on your behalf. You understand that any resulting medical care or transportation is your responsibility.

6. Studio Conduct & Safety

You agree to follow all instructor guidance and use equipment as directed, and to notify the instructor of any concerns or safety issues. Your safety is a shared responsibility.

7. Media Consent

Photos or videos may occasionally be taken during class for promotional use. By participating, you consent to the use of your image unless you notify the instructor in advance.

8. Agreement & Legal Terms

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario, Canada. If any part of this agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining sections will continue to apply.

Final Acknowledgment: By signing below, you confirm that you have read and understand this agreement, you are voluntarily participating, and you understand that you are waiving certain legal rights, including the right to bring a claim for injuries or damages arising from your participation, including those related to negligence (except in cases of gross negligence or intentional harm).

Hi, I'm Amanda.

I'm a certified Pilates instructor and the founder of Red Maple Movement. I'm trained in both mat and reformer Pilates, and currently offer small-group mat classes focused on intentional, empowering movement that feels accessible and refined.

I came to Pilates through my own experience and quickly felt the impact of focused, mindful movement — not just in strength and stability, but in how you carry yourself day to day. That perspective continues to shape how I teach.

Each class is designed with care to create a supportive, considered environment where you can move with purpose and build lasting strength. Whether you're new to Pilates, returning after time away, or already have a practice, you're welcome here.
